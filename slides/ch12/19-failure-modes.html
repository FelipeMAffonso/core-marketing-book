Five recurring failure modes share one test. Every AI retail claim needs a measured failure mode.

  • Pure-tech, no human fallback. Just Walk Out at grocery scale routed ~700 of 1,000 baskets to human review.
  • DTC crossover without the unit economics. Casper and Allbirds opened stores SoHo rent could not carry.
  • Mid-market stagnation. Macy's is eaten from below and above at once.
  • Generic-format-fit. JCPenney's switch and Walmart Germany both transplanted a format the buyer did not want.
  • AI with no measured failure mode. The vendor names no cost when the model is wrong.

The detector · ask three questions

For any retail-tech or AI claim, the test that separated Just Walk Out at the stadium from Just Walk Out at the supermarket.

  1. What happens the day the model is wrong?
  2. What is the human-fallback path?
  3. What does a false positive cost at scale?

When the answer to all three is "the vendor does not say," the claim is still a pilot, whatever the brochure calls it.
