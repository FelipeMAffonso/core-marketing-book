Choosing what not to carry is a marketing decision, not a logistics one. Fewer items push per-SKU velocity an order of magnitude higher.

The three-step logic

Narrow assortment is one decision that makes the next two possible.

Each step buys the next

  • Concentrate volume, so per-SKU velocity runs ~10x higher
  • That volume buys supplier leverage on cost
  • Which funds private label at ~80% of the shelf
