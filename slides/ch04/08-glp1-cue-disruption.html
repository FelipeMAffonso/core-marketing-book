A drug that damps the appetite cue cut grocery spend most where the category was built on the cue itself.

Exhibit 4.4 · Grocery-spend change within six months of GLP-1 adoption · by category

Total spend fell 5.3%. Savory snacks and fast-food runs, the categories built on the cue-reward loop, fell hardest, and higher-income households cut more than lower-income did.

The mechanism

The cue is still in the store; the signal that used to trigger the buy is damped at the source.

Why it generalizes

  • About one in four U.S. households now has a member on a prescription.
  • Mondelez North America reported a 4.1% net revenue decline in early 2026.
  • A brand built on a single cue is exposed the moment the cue disappears.
