Run the new-shelf audit on a brand you actually buy.

01 Map its category entry points. List the times, places, moods, and activities where the brand comes to mind for you. That list is its mental availability inside your head.
02 Ask the AI shelf. Have ChatGPT or Claude recommend a category for one of those situations, three picks with a reason each, the way a buyer would ask.
03 Find the gap. Is the brand on the model's list? If not, you have found a mental-availability gap, the brand is invisible at that moment to the model that is now half of memory.
04 Name one fix. Pick one entry point the brand could plausibly own, and one distinctive asset, a shape, a color, a sound, recognizable inside the eight-second scroll.
