Wendy's reversed in twelve days; the Indianapolis Zoo's grid held, because its off-peak floor genuinely fell.

Wendy's · Feb 2024

Reversed within twelve days

Announced a peak-hour surcharge on staple food framed as a margin tool, with no off-peak discount.

  • Read as gouging a captive lunch crowd
  • Viral "surge pricing" backlash and a 6.4% stock drop
Indianapolis Zoo · 2009

Replaced one price with a published grid

Swapped the single $16.95 adult price for an $8 Tuesday floor to a $30 Saturday peak, posted in advance.

  • Budget families paid less than the old single price
  • Weekday share moved 57% to 67%; revenue rose 12%

Source: Wendy's Q4 2023 earnings call, CNBC/WSJ (Feb 2024); Pricing Solutions, Indianapolis Zoo case (2010)
