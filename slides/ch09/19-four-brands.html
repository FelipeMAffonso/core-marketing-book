Four brands you know solve consistent service at scale, each through the discipline a different trait forces.

Singapore Airlines · variability ~4 mo Cabin-crew induction, twice the industry. About $70M a year on training buys reliability.
Costco · perishability 92.3% US and Canada renewal, FY2025. The membership itself is the renewable annual product.
Patagonia · the goods line 174,799 Garments repaired in FY2025. Worn Wear layers a service onto the jacket.
Mayo Clinic · intangibility 1 Value run as the operating rule. It anchors care the buyer cannot inspect.

In each case the company is the brand, because direct experience of the service is what gives the brand its meaning.

Source: Kotler, Keller & Chernev 16e (2022); Costco FY2025 Annual Report; Patagonia 2025 Impact Report
