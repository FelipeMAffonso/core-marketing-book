A well-resolved failure is associated with more loyalty than an encounter where nothing went wrong.

Exhibit 9.5 · Buyer loyalty by what happened in the encounter · directional pattern from the recovery literature

The buyer who watches the firm put a mistake right gets evidence the no-problem buyer never sees.

So what

Compensation buys the resolution, and the recovery itself buys the evidence the firm shows up under stress.

The effect is conditional

  • Severity: mild reverses, severe does not
  • Frequency: first-time recovers, a repeat reads as a pattern
  • Locus: a timed-out system forgives, stable incompetence does not

Source: Tax, Brown & Chandrashekaran, J. of Marketing (1998); conditions per Smith, Bolton & Wagner (1999)
