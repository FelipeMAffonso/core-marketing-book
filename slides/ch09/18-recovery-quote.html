The recovery rule · interactional justice over the dollar amount

In the agentic era the rule sorts the work. An AI front door took the routine inquiries that used to fill about 70 percent of front-line phone minutes at Marriott in 2024, and a human tier kept the 25 to 30 percent with high emotional stakes. A field study of 5,179 support agents found the productivity gain held only where a human tier sat underneath, because the utilitarian buyer wants speed while the hedonic buyer wants to feel heard.
