Front-line authority during the encounter is the recovery system, so a recovery has to match the shape of the failure.

Ritz-Carlton · the right shape

The $2,000 cap is the system

Every employee may spend up to $2,000 per day per guest to fix a problem, without manager approval.

  • The cap is rarely hit; knowing it exists is the point
  • The stained coat came back with a note, a $40 transaction retold for a decade
  • The line cook, valet, and housekeeper can act at the encounter
Carnival Triumph · the wrong shape

A uniform package, after the fact

Passengers received $500, a refund, and a free flight from corporate after four days adrift.

  • No front-line authority during the failure
  • A severe failure sits outside the recoverable band
  • A generous refund does not reverse it

Source: Kotler, Keller & Chernev, Marketing Management 16e (2022); Carnival Triumph reporting (2013)
