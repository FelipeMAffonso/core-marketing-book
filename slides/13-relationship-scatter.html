Price sensitivity tracks income, but three segments break the pattern.

Exhibit · Price sensitivity index vs. median household income (r = 0.62)

Gray is the trend. Coral marks the three outlier segments worth naming.

How to read

Correlation, not causation. The three coral dots are the ones we want to explain.

Design moves

  • Axes labeled with units.
  • Fit line only when r justifies it.
  • Coral the outliers, not the cloud.
  • Name the outliers directly on the chart.

Source: Consumer panel · n = 4,200 · Pearson r = 0.62 · correlation ≠ causation
