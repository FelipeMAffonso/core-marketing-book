Komatsu · the price attack

A 40% cost advantage, no share gain

Through the 1980s Komatsu pushed hard on a roughly 40% lower unit price.

  • The buyer still had to weigh losing parts within 24 hours
  • The local importer could not match Cat Financial terms
Caterpillar · belief-based power

156 dealers held the network

Cat runs about 156 multi-generational dealers across 190-plus countries on expert and referent power.

  • 24-hour parts fulfillment on critical components
  • About $15–17B a year of financing through Cat Financial

Source: Kotler, Keller & Chernev, Marketing Management 16e (2022); Caterpillar dealer-network case; downtime costs the buyer thousands of dollars an hour
