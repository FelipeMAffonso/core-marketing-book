Run the one-page channel audit on a brand you actually shop.

01 Name its channels and place it on the ladder. List where it sells, then decide whether it is exclusive, selective, or intensive, and the one pressure pushing it toward more outlets.
02 Score the six omni-channel components. Rate each retail partner zero to two on single inventory, pickup, ship-from-store, returns parity, one record, and one price ladder per SKU.
03 Price it backward through the channel. Feed each tier its margin down from the shelf price, keeping markup and margin straight, then show what selling direct would leave you.
04 Flag one conflict and write each channel's role. Name one move to add or cut, then hand it to an assistant that searches the live web and read the sharp answer against the vague one.
