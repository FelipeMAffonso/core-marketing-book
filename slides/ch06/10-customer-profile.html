No platform sells a "treats hydration as identity" checkbox, so you translate the strategy into descriptors you can buy.

Strategic segment · how a strategist thinks

"Women 25 to 44 who treat hydration as part of who they are."

Customer profile · what a planner can target
  • TikTok users in US metros above 250,000
  • Household income above $60,000
  • Prior engagement with wellness or fitness content

Score it two ways that pull against each other. Effectiveness reaches the buyers you chose; cost efficiency reaches only those, not a much larger crowd who share the descriptors and waste the spend.

Source: Kotler, Keller & Chernev, Marketing Management 16e (2022), Ch. 6; Palmatier & Sridhar (2021)
