A big, growing, profitable segment can still be wrong. Score compatibility before attractiveness.

The size trap

What tempts you

The slice is large and full of money, so it looks obvious.

  • A small water brand eyes the mass bottled-water market
  • The segment passes every test of size and growth
  • It walks into Coca-Cola's distribution and loses
The win test · strategic step

What to ask first

Two questions about you and the segment together.

  • Compatibility: can you serve them better than rivals can?
  • Attractiveness: do they create more value for you than another slice?
  • If a stronger rival already serves them better, walk away

Source: Kotler, Keller & Chernev, Marketing Management 16e (2022), Ch. 6; Chernev (2020)
