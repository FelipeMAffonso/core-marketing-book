Carry one four-step diagnostic into the client project. Run it on a brand you actually buy.

01 Name the segment in one plain sentence. If you cannot, the brand is chasing nobody in particular, which is the everybody trap.
02 Score that segment on the five criteria. Measurable, accessible, substantial, differentiable, actionable. Fail one and it is research.
03 Write the position. For this target who has this need, the brand is the one thing no rival can match. Cut any clause a rival could say word for word.
04 Place it on a map. Plot the brand and two rivals on the two attributes the buyer cares about, find the empty quadrant, then ask whether any buyers want to live there.
