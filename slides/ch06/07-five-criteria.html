A slice is only a usable segment when it passes five tests. Fail one, and it is research.

1

Measurable

You can size it from data you can actually get.

2

Accessible

You can reach it with a marketing program.

3

Substantial

It is large and profitable enough to repay serving it.

4

Differentiable

It responds differently than other segments do.

5

Actionable

The firm has the resources to run a program for it.

→

The honesty check

If you cannot describe it in one plain sentence, it is a statistical artifact.

Source: Wedel & Kamakura (2000); Day, Strategic Mgmt. J. (1981); Wind, JMR (1978)
