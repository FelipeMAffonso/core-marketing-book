The buyer ranks a handful of brands per category, and the top rung is the default answer.

Exhibit 6.3 · A mental ladder · the cola category

You carry one ladder per category. Three rungs is normal; seven is the ceiling. A brand with no clear claim never makes the list, and being on top is what keeps a brand on top.

The cheapest move is to claim the rung the buyer already handed you. VW launched the Beetle in 1959 under "Think small," and by 1965 held 67% of the US imported-car market.
