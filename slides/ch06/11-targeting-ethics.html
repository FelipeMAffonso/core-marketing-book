Target's 2012 pregnancy-prediction case

The same machinery that finds the buyer you want can find buyers you have no right to single out. Three guardrails: do not target proxies for a protected trait (each variable can be lawful while the combination is not), COPPA applies the moment minors fall inside the segment, and steering prices off a behavioral read crosses into manipulation (Orbitz, 2012). Since 2018, the GDPR and US state laws fence this in.
