The decoy effect

A dominated option pulls choice toward the option that dominates it.

Cinema popcorn · share of buyers by menu

The setup

  • Two strong options, one trade-off
  • A dominated third option
  • Buyer selects the option that dominates the decoy

Where you see it

  • Cinema popcorn pricing
  • SaaS three-tier pricing
  • Apple storage tiers

Source: Huber, Payne & Puto, JCR (1982); asymmetric-dominance experimental paradigm.
