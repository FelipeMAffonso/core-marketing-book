Uber surge vs Wendy's

The same dynamic logic produces an opposite reception.

Uber surge

Fare rises during a storm or a concert, against an imbalance between rider demand and driver supply.

Riders attribute the higher price to scarcity, which exceeds the fairness threshold.

launched 2012, tolerated for thirteen years

Wendy's dynamic menu

The announcement framed digital menu boards as a tool to raise prices at peak demand times.

Buyers interpreted it as charging the same person more for the identical item, the windfall pattern below the fairness threshold.

announced Feb 26, 2024; reversed Mar 9, 2024

Source: Kahneman, Knetsch & Thaler, AER (1986); Wendy's Q4 2023 call (Feb 26, 2024); clarification Mar 9, 2024.
