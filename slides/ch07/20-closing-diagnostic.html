Run the four-step audit on a brand you actually use.

01 Sort every cue on fame and uniqueness. Lock the load-bearing ones; retire the decoration.
02 Name the situations the brand owns. Pick the biggest doorway it is still missing.
03 Write the onliness sentence and run the swap test. Tighten it until only your brand fits.
04 Check the position is backed on four channels. The product, the supply chain, the hiring, the audited charity.
