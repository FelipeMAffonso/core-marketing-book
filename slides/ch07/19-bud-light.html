Bud Light, 2023 · ~$1.4B in lost revenue · lost the top-beer spot

A brand can credibly hold a cultural position only with resident standing it built before claiming it. Bud Light borrowed a world it had no record in. The instruction follows: take only positions you can verify on four channels the buyer can check independently, the product, the supply chain, the hiring record, and the audited charitable giving. Patagonia's environmental position is verifiable on all four; Bud Light's was verifiable on none.
