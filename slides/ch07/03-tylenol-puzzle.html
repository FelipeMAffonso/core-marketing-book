A 37% leader that recovers from a 30-point fall in a year. The puzzle the chapter answers: what must a brand be for that to happen?

The puzzle

Johnson & Johnson pulled 31 million capsules in eight days, at a cost above $100 million.

What we have to explain

  • What must a brand be for a 30-point drop to reverse?
  • Why does a rival with the same fast response never come back?
  • Perrier faced the same shock in 1990 and sold itself to Nestle.
