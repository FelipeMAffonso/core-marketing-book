Campaigns that recruit new buyers beat campaigns aimed at existing buyers about three to one on sales.

Exhibit 7.6 · IPA Effectiveness archive · sales lift per dollar, by campaign aim

Penetration drives growth: brands grow by adding buyers, not by squeezing more visits out of last year's.

So what

Loyalty programs lift frequency only slightly, because most members would have come back anyway.

The instruction

  • Fund reach before you fund frequency.
  • When a category shrinks, buyers leave it, not buy less.
  • Reaching new buyers is what mental availability buys.

Source: Binet & Field, IPA Effectiveness archive (996 campaigns, 30 years)
