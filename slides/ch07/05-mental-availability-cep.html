Mental availability

The probability that your brand comes to mind when a buying situation arrives.

How a brand grows

  • By being thought of in more situations, not remembered harder in one.
  • The rival can have a better-tasting burger and still lose.
  • Most of the buyer's moments never bring it to mind.

Source: Romaniuk & Sharp, How Brands Grow Part 2 (2016); category-entry-point construct
