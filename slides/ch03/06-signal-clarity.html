The clearer a signal gets, the fewer cheap moves you have left.

Exhibit 3.3 · Signal clarity rises while affordable responses fall · a draft becomes a comment period becomes a dated rule

So what

Most committees feel safe to act only once the rule has a date, and by then the cheap options are gone.

The skill

  • A draft buried in trade press costs a one-page contingency.
  • Act while the signal is still faint enough that almost nobody believes it.

Source: Ansoff, "Managing Strategic Surprise by Response to Weak Signals," California Management Review 18, no. 2 (1975)
