SWOT earns its keep only when scored: magnitude times importance ranks the agenda.

Exhibit 3.7 · A scored SWOT for Stanley, late 2025 · magnitude (−3 to +3) × importance (1 to 3)

So what

A plus-three strength buyers do not pay for scores only plus three, an impressive thing nobody funds.

From rank to move

  • Multiply the two scores; commit money only above four.
  • Cross the top strength with the top opportunity.
  • Brand assets (+9) × international (+6) names the UK and AU play.
