Sociocultural · adoption 1 in 8 US adults reported using a GLP-1 medication by late 2025. Habits and identity around food moved faster than any annual survey could track.
Economic · category demand −31% Traditional snack purchases in those households. The same shift presses on the sociocultural and economic axes together, which is why a one-axis read misses it.
