The environment splits by who you can argue with.

Microenvironment

Parties you can call, sign with, or sue

Customers, the company, collaborators, competitors, and publics. The muscle is signing contracts.

  • Stanley's buyer at Target negotiates shelf space.
  • A steel supplier sells on terms you renegotiate.
Macroenvironment

Forces you can only read and answer

Political, economic, sociocultural, technological, environmental, legal. The muscle is reading the world early.

  • You cannot phone TikTok and renegotiate its feed.
  • You cannot bargain with a recession or an EU rule.

The two layers ask for different muscles, so a team that only signs contracts still gets caught by the force it never learned to read.

Source: Ferrell, Hartline & Hochstein, Marketing Strategy 8e (2022), Ch. 3; Kotler, Keller & Chernev, Marketing Management 16e (2022), Ch. 2
