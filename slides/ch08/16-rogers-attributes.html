Five attributes are dials a product manager can turn, so adoption becomes a design checklist, not a wait.

Relative advantagehow much better than what it replaces raise A gain that dwarfs the switching loss, not a slight edge
Compatibilityfit with existing habits and values raise The Prius drove exactly like a gas car
Complexityhow hard to understand and use lower The AirPods hearing-aid switch needed no relearning
Trialabilityhow easily a buyer can test it raise Casper's 100-night guarantee on a mattress you cannot lie on
Observabilityhow visible the benefit is to others raise The Liquid Death can: the buyer carrying it advertises it

Source: Rogers, Diffusion of Innovations 5e (2003); Dolan, “Product Policy,” HBS Core Curriculum 8208 (2015)
