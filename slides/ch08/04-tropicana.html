Only the package moved. Loyal buyers scanning the shelf could no longer find the orange-and-straw cue.

So what

A consumer test that does not measure shelf-recognition is testing the wrong thing.

The skipped test

  • Engineering, visual, and dealer tests all passed.
  • It skipped whether buyers could find the brand in two seconds.
  • PepsiCo reverted within two months, about $35M lost.
