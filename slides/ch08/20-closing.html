Carry four moves into any product in front of you.

01 Decompose the offer on its three elements. What does the buyer touch, what does the firm do around it, what does the buyer already believe? Name the weakest leg.
02 Place it on its life-cycle stage. Read the sales trend, the saturation, and the competitors, then ask which job that stage hands you. The curve is a diagnostic, not a schedule.
03 Run the stage-gate kill-thresholds. Score desirability, feasibility, and viability before the spend turns irreversible. Clearing two of three is not ready.
04 Audit any extension for brand-fit. Name what the parent means and what the category requires, then cut the move where the two conflict, the way Bic perfume should have been cut.
