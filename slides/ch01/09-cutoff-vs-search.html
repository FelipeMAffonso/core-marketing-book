The model itself · knowledge cutoff Frozen on a fixed day

It learned from a huge pile of reading that stopped on its cutoff date, like a student who crammed the whole library up to one day and never read again.

The app around it · web search Reads the live page

Ask about a phone that launched last week and the app searches on its own, reads what it finds, and answers from that, so you can treat it as up to date.

Switch search off and the model drops back to whatever it memorized, then produces a fluent guess about anything newer. The current answer lives in the search step, not the frozen weights.
