Four brands, four stopping points; the rung tells you what each trusts a machine to do.

Liquid Death Rung 1 · ask Low on purpose. AI riffs on ad concepts, but a person writes the voice, because the product it sells is the attitude.
Costco Rung 1 · ask, quiet Back office only. No chatbot, no launch. A model helps the bakery forecast. You read the rung from the silence.
Patagonia Rung 3 · connect Connected, voice stays human. AI reaches its supply-chain data to measure its footprint, by stated policy.
Duolingo Rung 4 · schedule Rebuilt around overnight AI. It grades and writes fresh lessons while users sleep. The 2025 memo drew a backlash.

The rung a brand picks is a decision about where a machine helps and where a person has to stay.
