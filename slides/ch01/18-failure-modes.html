The pull underneath

The model answers in a calm, finished voice whether it is right or wrong, and that confidence quietly invites you to skip the check. Leaning on an answer because it sounds sure is AI overreliance. One guard rides along: keep a customer list or anything confidential out of a public chatbot, the way Samsung leaked source code in 2023. Ch26 carries both further.
