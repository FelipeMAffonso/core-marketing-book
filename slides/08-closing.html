Four things to carry into every chart you make.

01 Write the title as a sentence. If you can't, the slide isn't ready.
02 Gray context, coral claim. One accent per slide, on the one thing worth saying.
03 Direct labels beat legends. Three lookups become one.
04 Always disclose the base. A percentage without n is a claim without evidence.
