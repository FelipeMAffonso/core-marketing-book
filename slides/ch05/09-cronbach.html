A scale below 0.70 on Cronbach's alpha is mostly reading static, so settle it before you act on a two-point drop.

Exhibit 5.5 · Cronbach's alpha · share of the score that is real signal, two scales vs the 0.70 floor

Alpha runs zero to one: zero means the questions are unrelated, one means they are redundant. An 0.84 scale is about 84% signal; an 0.59 scale is mostly noise.

Why one question is not enough

One item mixes the thing you care about with whatever the wording stirred up that day.

The fix

  • Ask four or five questions circling the same idea, then average them.
  • The noise cancels and the real signal stacks up.
  • Below the floor two quarters running, pause the call and rebuild the scale.
