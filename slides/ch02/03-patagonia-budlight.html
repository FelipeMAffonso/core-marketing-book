One brand survived a $3B giveaway. The other could not survive a single can. Coherence is the only difference.

Patagonia · 2022

Gave the company away, kept selling

Roughly $3 billion of stock moved into a climate trust, and revenue held the next morning.

  • Durable gear, the climber who hates waste, a price that covers it
  • Few stores, field-story ads, every decision pointed one way
Bud Light · 2023

Approved one promotion, lost the lead

The top US beer for 22 years lost the spot in about eight weeks and logged $1B+ in lost sales.

  • One promotion aimed at a buyer the other four did not serve
  • Down close to 30% by early 2026, sitting third

Source: Patagonia ownership transfer (Sep 2022); AB InBev / Bud Light (Apr 2023–2026), cross-confirmed by CNN, CBS News
