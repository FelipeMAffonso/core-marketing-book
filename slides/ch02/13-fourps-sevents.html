Why the split is the upgrade

Crammed into one word, a weekend coupon eats the budget meant to build the brand.

What each split separates

  • Product: the artifact, the service around it, the brand in memory.
  • Promotion: the long work of building, the short work of a sale.
  • A brand is distinctive cues, not a claimed difference.
