The firm kept the distinctive green look and stopped over-targeting the construction worker. It did not narrow harder; it let mental and physical availability scale to the buyer the cup already served.

The trigger

A women-run account bought 5,000 Quenchers and sold out in five days.

What the new president did

  • Read the sellout as the cup's real buyer, ignored for a century.
  • Wardrobe-matched colors, Target distribution, TikTok over magazine ads.
  • Kept the distinctive assets, scaled availability to her.
