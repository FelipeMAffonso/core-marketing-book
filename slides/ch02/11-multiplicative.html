Marketing is multiplicative, not additive, so four good decisions and one broken one do not average into a passing plan.

Why coherence is the game

The four good decisions pay to deliver the buyer straight to the friction the fifth created.

The strategy name for it

  • Fit: each choice reinforces the others.
  • Trade-off: each choice accepts not doing something else.
  • A narrow segment served perfectly is still too small to grow on.
