Accessibility · WCAG 2.2 AA

Compliant by default

This courseware is built to the same bar as the textbook: it ships conformant, then layers further accommodations on top. The reader includes high-contrast, large-text, reduced-motion, and dyslexia-friendly font modes.

  • Color contrast meets WCAG AA 4.5:1 for text; coral is routed to a darker terracotta (#a8472a) wherever it carries small text.
  • Every interactive element is a real link or button with a visible focus ring and a target size of at least 24×24 px.
  • Decorative icons are hidden from assistive tech; planned items are announced as unavailable, not just grayed visually.
  • Motion respects prefers-reduced-motion; the layout reflows cleanly down to a 320 px viewport.
