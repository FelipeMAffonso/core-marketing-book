What has shipped

Everything below is live and accessible. Items labeled "draft" are reachable and usable but are still being polished or extended across all chapters.

Read & Reference

  • Textbook v7 Available 27 chapters

    All 27 chapters, each readable at two depths (Comprehensive and Concise) with interactive AI widgets, hand-drawn coral SVG illustrations, and a full references apparatus. A toggleable redline layer shows every addition over the prior version with an inline rationale, so adopting instructors can track what changed and why.

  • Glossary Available A–Z

    Every key term across all 27 chapters, defined in plain language and cross-linked back to the chapter where it first appears.

  • AI Prompt Library Draft 27 chapters

    Copy-paste prompts that turn each chapter's framework into a hands-on AI exercise. The library was compiled recently from the in-chapter AI Apply steps; the full formatted page is in draft.

Teach

  • Slide Decks Draft 9 of 27

    Chapter-level lecture decks built on the book's coral design system, one claim per slide, with the chapter's SVG illustrations lifted in as visual anchors. Chapters 2, 3, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, and 11 are complete. The remaining 18 are in progress.

  • Teaching Notes / Instructor Guide Draft 3 of 27

    Session-by-session instructor guides covering timing, talking points, discussion prompts, common misconceptions, and the hands-on AI moment for each chapter. Chapters 6 (STP), 7 (Brands), and 10 (Pricing) are complete with minute-by-minute plans and bundle references. The remaining 24 are in the queue.

  • Lecture Videos Draft / In production Scripts: 3 of 27 written

    Short animated lecture videos (the HBR-explainer format). Chapter 10 (Pricing) has a complete rendered video. Scripts for Chapters 2, 6, and 7 are finished and verified; narration generation and rendering follow once the voice budget is confirmed. The production pipeline (66 reusable Remotion worlds, ElevenLabs alignment, PRODUCTION BIBLE) is fully documented and proven.

  • Cases Draft 1 of 27

    Teaching cases with discussion questions tied to the chapter's running brand example. The Chapter 6 STP case (Stanley/brand repositioning) is complete, with a template for the remaining chapters.

Assess

  • Test and Quiz Bank Draft 27 of 27 chapters, ~760 items

    All 27 chapters covered. Items are adapted from the Kotler MM16 bank and written from scratch for the AI-era chapters that have no Kotler equivalent. Each item is re-tagged to the book's own learning objectives, AACSB-tagged (Application / Reflective / Analytical / Ethics), and QTI-formatted for export. This is a full draft; review and editorial polish are still in progress.

  • Assignments Draft 2 of 27

    Graded individual take-home assignments tied to each chapter's learning objectives, with AI-as-critic use built in and rubrics mapped to the real LOs. Chapters 6 and 10 are complete. A reusable template is in place for the remaining chapters.

Adopt & Deploy

  • Syllabus and Pacing Guide Draft

    An adaptable syllabus with 34 clearly marked [ADAPT] placeholders for institution, course number, and term details. Includes a 15-week pacing of all 27 chapters, course-level outcomes, an AACSB-tag note, and a modern AI-use policy. No invented institution or course number. Ready to adapt and teach from.

  • Canvas / LMS Cartridge Draft 804 items, QTI 1.2 / Common Cartridge

    A Common Cartridge (.imscc) generated from the 27-chapter test bank: 662 auto-graded MCQ items and 142 manually graded essay and AI-era items, organized by chapter with a full imsmanifest.xml. Import directly into Canvas, Moodle, D2L, or any IMS-compatible LMS. Draft status reflects that the underlying test bank is still being polished editorially.

  • AACSB Outcomes Map Draft

    A 27-chapter master map grounded in the real AACSB tags on the test-bank items (805 tag occurrences: Application 368, Reflective 223, Analytical 183, Ethics 25). Covers MCQ tiers, AI-era items, and projects P1-P3, with a closing-the-loop assurance-of-learning cycle and [ADAPT] placeholders for program-level competencies.

  • Accessibility / VPAT Available WCAG 2.2 AA

    The courseware ships compliant by default. All text contrast meets WCAG AA 4.5:1; coral accent is routed to a darker terracotta (#a8472a) wherever it carries small text. Every control is a real link or button with a visible focus ring. High-contrast, large-text, reduced-motion, and dyslexia-friendly font modes are available through the reader settings.
